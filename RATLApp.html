Teaching and Learning Map

Core Capabilities

Curriculum Management

Curriculum Design

Design learning units and paths of study to enable learners to acquire competencies based on the enterprise's market position.

Scheduling

Manage and schedule resources, such as instructors, facilities, and tools.

Instructor Management

Manage the pool of instructors and facilitate activities such as shared governance or design of learning and assessment resources.

Matriculation and Enrollment

Onboard new learners for one or more learning units.

Instructional Design

Learning Component Design

Design new learning components.

Learning Component Production

Produce new learning components based on learning component designs.

Learning Component Curation

Store, re-use, and archive learning components.

Learning Component Assessment

Assess learning components before and after use.

Accreditation

Curriculum Review

Review and revise the enterprise's activities to meet accreditation standards.

Accreditation Reporting

Report on the enterprise's activities in support of accreditation.

Teaching

Scaffolding

Plan a learning experience, such as a course.

Learner Management

View, communicate with, and group the learners enrolled in a learning unit instance.

Learning Component Delivery

Make learning components available for learning.

Learning

Learning Plan

Plan the learner's progress, based on the learner's goals for applying their acquired competencies.

Mentoring/Advising

Advise learners based on their goals, learning plan, and assessment results. May also include proactive advising in the form of intervention.

Learning Experience

Progress toward desired competencies through interaction with an instructor, learning component, and/or other learners.

Group Collaboration

Enable learners to work together and produce artifacts in groups.

Learner Content Management

Store and re-use the artifacts produced by learners.

Portfolio Management

Enable learners to organize their work in a professional portfolio.

Assessment

Assessment Design

Plan and design assessments based on curriculum goals.

Assessment Administration

Carry out assessment of learners.

Assessment Evaluation

Evaluate the results of learner assessment.

Learner Certification

Grant statuses (such as degrees, certificates, badges) to learners who have demonstrated competencies through assessment.

Supporting Capabilities

Academic Records Administration

Maintain records including matriculations, enrollments, instructor assignments, assessment results, and learner certifications.

Human Resources Administration

Recruit and retain human resources including instructors, staff, and student employees.

Identity and Access Management

Maintain identity records to enable participants to access appropriate enterprise resources and participate in inter-enterprise identity federations.

Technology Management

Review and revise the array of technology solutions that support enterprise capabilities.

Data Analytics

Enable analytic reporting on all teaching and learning data, for example, for enrollment management, accreditation reporting, and compliance reporting.

Communication and Collaboration

Support physical and virtual avenues of communication and collaboration between instructors, learners, and other participants.

Facilities Management

Maintain information about space and related resources to support teaching and learning.

Financials Management

Perform budgeting and other planning to support teaching and learning initiatives.
